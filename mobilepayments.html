Business To Client(B2C)API

We can intergrate your systems wB2C API is an API used to make payments from a Business to Customers (Pay Outs), also known as Bulk Disbursements. B2C API is used in several scenarios by businesses that require to either make Salary Payments, Cashback payments, Promotional Payments(e.g. betting winning payouts), winnings, financial institutions withdrawal of funds, loan disbursements, etc.


Business to Business (B2B)API

It allows businesses to transfer money directly to each other's M-Pesa accounts securely and in real-time.
M-Pesa B2B is designed to make it easy for businesses to make payments to their suppliers, partners, and distributors. It eliminates the need for physical cash transactions, reducing the risk of loss or theft.
To use M-Pesa B2B, businesses need to register for the service and have an active M-Pesa account. They can then transfer funds to other M-Pesa accounts by entering the recipient's phone number and the amount they wish to transfer.
M-Pesa B2B has become a popular payment solution for businesses in Kenya, and it has helped to transform the country's economy by making financial transactions faster, cheaper, and more secure.


Customer-to-Business (C2B)AP1

Trefers to M-Pesa Customer-to-Business, a service offered by Safaricom's M-Pesa mobile payment platform in Kenya. It allows customers to make payments directly to businesses through their M-Pesa accounts.
M-Pesa C2B is designed to make it easy for customers to make payments for goods and services without the need for physical cash transactions. It allows customers to pay for goods and services using their mobile phones, which is more convenient and secure than carrying cash.
To use M-Pesa C2B, customers need to have an active M-Pesa account and sufficient funds in their account to make the payment. They can then make payments to businesses by entering the business's PayBill number, the account number, and the amount they wish to pay.
M-Pesa C2B has become a popular payment solution for businesses in Kenya, especially for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). It has helped SMEs to grow by providing them with a simple and secure way to receive payments from customers.


M-Pesa STK (Short-Term Key) push

This is a service provided by Safaricom's M-Pesa mobile payment platform in Kenya. It allows businesses to receive payments from customers through an integrated payment gateway on their website or mobile app.
When a customer selects the M-Pesa STK push option on a business's website or app, they are prompted to enter their M-Pesa PIN on their mobile phone. Once the PIN is entered, the payment is processed in real-time, and the customer receives an instant confirmation message.
M-Pesa STK push is a convenient and secure way for businesses to receive payments from customers. It eliminates the need for customers to manually enter the PayBill or BuyGoods numbers, which can reduce errors and improve the customer experience. It also helps to reduce the risk of fraud by providing a secure and encrypted payment channel.
Overall, M-Pesa STK push has transformed the way businesses in Kenya receive payments, and it has helped to create a more inclusive and accessible financial system in the country.
