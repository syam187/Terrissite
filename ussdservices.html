USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) is a technology used by mobile network operators to provide interactive and menu-driven services to mobile phone users.
USSD services are typically accessed by dialing a specific code (often starting with a "*") on a mobile phone, followed by a series of numbers or characters, and then pressing the "send" or "call" button.

Mobile Payments

We can intergrate your systems with USSD which is commonly used for mobile banking services, allowing users to perform transactions such as checking account balances, transferring funds, paying bills, and more, directly from their mobile phones.

Credit top-up/Information services

USSD is often used to recharge or top-up mobile airtime credits by dialing a specific code and following the prompts.
Hence we are able to credit a user with the right amount they requested

Mobile content services

We have platforms aided by USSD that access mobile content services such as marketing of services, football predictions, and other digital content.

Customer service/Polling and voting

USSD can be used to access customer support services, such as checking account status, reporting network issues, and getting help with mobile phone settings.
USSD can be used for conducting voting or polling campaigns, allowing users to cast their votes or opinions via USSD codes.
