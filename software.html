Business Softwares

This refers to computer programs and applications designed to assist in managing various aspects of a business. There are numerous types of business software available, each serving a specific purpose to help streamline business operations and improve efficiency. Some common types of business software we have created include include

Accounting software

Helps businesses manage their financial transactions.

Including recording and tracking income and expenses.

Generating financial reports, and managing payroll.

Betting Platforms

Used to manage financial transactions that is deposits and withdraws

Managing users profile, providing a platform to place predictions.

Providing bonuses, and instant payments.

Communication and collaboration software

Facilitates communication and collaboration among team members.

Include email,instant messaging and video conferencing.

Aid in document sharing(Large files) and project management tools.

E-commerce Softwares

Softwares to enable businesses to sell products or sell services online.

Include onlne storefronts and shopping cart functionality.

Wide payments options that include Visa, Mpesa options, pesapal, paypal.skrill and many more.

Human Resources Management System (HRMS) software

Aid business will large human resources processes.

Include employee onboarding, payroll management and attendance tracking.

Help in performance tracking and benefits administration.

Business intelligence (BI) software

Softwares that helps analyze and interpret data to gain insights

It includes tools for data visualization, data reporting, and data analyses.

Make informed decisions on behalf of the company hence enhancing well conducted researches.
